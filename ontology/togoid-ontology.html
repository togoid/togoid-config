Datatype properties

tio:display_label (rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty )
rdfs:comment A label to be displayed on the TogoID web UI.

TogoID is a service developed by the Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS) for converting identifiers of databases in life sciences. TogoID ontology (TIO) is developed for semantically annotate relations in the conversion.
